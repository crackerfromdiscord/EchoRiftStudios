🦍 What is ApeTech?

ApeTech is a Gorilla Tag horror game made by Coral, JuneBug, Noah, and IHasAFaceLULZ. It is currently in its Beta/Alpha stage and doesn't really offer much right now—but when it fully releases, it will have a ton to offer!

🏚️ What is Ashwood Lodge?

Ashwood Lodge is a creepy, atmospheric Gorilla Tag-meets-Doors inspired game. You’re an investigator sent to uncover the secrets of a lodge that’s much bigger—and darker—than it seems. Discover hidden rooms, survive horrors, and escape… if you can.
